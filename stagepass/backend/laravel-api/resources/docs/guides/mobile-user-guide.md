# Stagepass Mobile App – User Guide

Guide for crew and team leaders using the Stagepass mobile app.

---

## 1. Logging in

- Open the Stagepass mobile app.
- Enter your **email** and **password** (or use PIN if enabled).
- Tap **Sign in**. You will see your assigned events or a message that you have no event today.

---

## 2. Viewing your event

- From the home screen you see **Today's event** (if you have one assigned).
- You'll see event name, date, time, location, and who the team leader is.
- Tap the event to open **Event details**.

---

## 3. Check-in and check-out

### Check-in

- You must be **within the event's geofence** (set by the organiser) to check in.
- Open the event and tap **Check in**.
- Allow location access when prompted. Your position is sent to the server.
- If you're inside the geofence, check-in succeeds and the time is recorded.
- If you're outside, you'll see a message to move closer to the event location.

### Check-out

- When you're done, open the event and tap **Check out**.
- Your total hours are calculated from check-in to check-out and stored for payments.

**Note:** Team leaders or admins can also record **manual check-in** for you from the web admin if you can't use the app.

---

## 4. Time off requests

- From the app menu, go to **Time off** (or equivalent).
- Tap **Request time off**.
- Choose **Start date** and **End date**, add a reason if required.
- Submit. The request goes to admins for approval or rejection. You can see status in the app.

---

## 5. Payments

- Payments are created and approved in the **web admin** (by team leaders or admins) based on your hours and event work.
- In the app you may see **payment status** (e.g. pending, approved) for your events.
- If your organisation allows, you might be able to view or confirm payment details in the app; otherwise this is done in the web admin.

---

## 6. Notifications

- You may receive **notifications** for:
  - Being added to an event
  - Reminders before an event
  - Time off approved/rejected
  - Other alerts from your organisation
- Keep notifications enabled so you don't miss event or approval updates.

---

## 7. Support

- For login or app issues, contact your organisation's admin or support email.
- For feature requests or bugs, report them to your admin; they can pass feedback to the Stagepass team.

---

*When you change mobile flows (check-in, time off, payments, notifications), update this guide and commit it with your code changes.*
